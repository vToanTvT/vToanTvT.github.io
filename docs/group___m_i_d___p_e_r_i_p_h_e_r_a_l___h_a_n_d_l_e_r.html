Detailed Description

Implements relay control for LLC and PFC using GPIO abstraction.

Version
1.0
Date
2025-10-17
Author
EVSELab

This module provides control interfaces for system power peripherals:

  • LLC (Link Layer Converter) relay management
  • PFC (Power Factor Correction) relay management
  • Safety interlocking for power stage sequencing

The handler ensures proper timing and interlocking of relay operations:

  1. Safety verification before activation
  2. Sequenced switching between PFC and LLC stages
  3. Real-time feedback and relay status monitoring
Note
All relay operations include built-in safety checks and enforced timing to prevent damage to power components.
